Macaroni Bechamel Recipe

Description

One of the most iconic dishes in Egyptian cuisine, yet it is made with simple ingredients you can find in your nearby grocery store.

A comfort food favorite, and a crowd-pleaser dish that is a hit with both kids and adults.

It’s a great option for potlucks, gatherings, or a weeknight dinner since it reheats beautifully.

Ingredients

  • pasta: Usually, penne pasta
  • chopped onions
  • ground beef
  • tomato paste
  • Unsalted butter
  • oil
  • flour
  • milk
  • broth
  • Salt & pepper
  • ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions and start preparing the ground beef mixture.

    Saute onion, then add beef, break it apart and keep cooking until no longer pink. Add spices, tomato paste and mix all well.

  2. In another large pot melt butter, then add oil and flour.

    Whisk flour until it turns pale yellow.

  3. Gradually pour in milk and stock while whisking until the mixture has no lumps.

    season with salt and pepper and keep cooking until it thickens.

    Your bechamel is ready when it fully coats the back of a spoon and when you draw a path across with your finger it doesn’t disappear.

  4. Mix about ¾ of the bechamel sauce with the cooked pasta in the pasta pot.

  5. Cover the bottom of a 9x13 casserole dish with about ½ cup of the bechamel sauce.

    Spread half of the cooked pasta then top with the meat mixture.

  6. Spread the remaining pasta over then top with the bechamel sauce

  7. Level and bake in the center of your oven until top is golden brown.

    Let the casserole stand for 10-15 minutes before cutting and serving.
